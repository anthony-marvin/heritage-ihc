About us

At Heritage Integrative Healthcare, we offer simple, proven, and effective therapy for a wide range of muscular and joint problems. We combine traditional chiropractic methods with complementary therapies to ensure the highest treatment outcomes. As a specialty clinic, we often see very complex problems, including cases where the patient has multiple medical specialists. Our integrative team of doctors is trained to provide you with all the right therapies, advice, education, and resources to feel better now and return to the life you enjoy. 

Heritage IHC has been serving Falmouth and the Coastal Maine region since 2002.

The Team

Drs. Fox and Carroll, along with physical therapist, Rita Fox and acupuncturist, Meghan Cowens are crossed trained in orthopedics, neurology, Chiropractic, physical therapy, laser, acupuncture, Chinese medicine and soft tissue release, unlike any other group.
